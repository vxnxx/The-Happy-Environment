CLIMBING PLANTS

Vine, kiwi or ivy are among the climbing plants to be favored in an edible forest. With the blackberry, they are the champions of the race to the sun thanks to their spikes, thorns or tendrils, but are also very productive.


Take a look at our selection of climbing plants !

POTTED PLANTS

Potted Plants, large or small, have some strengths in common. For example, the fact that they can be planted at any time during the year (excluding frost and heat waves) or the possibility of not planting them immediately after their purchase.


Take a look at our selection of potted plants !

BUNCH OF FLOWERS

Bunches of flowers are assemblies of different varieties of flowers whose size can vary. They are arranged in pretty ways and allow to convey a message according to the meaning of each flower. So be careful with your mixes! Pleasant and ephemeral, flowers have psychological effects, they soothe us, and make us more sociable.To offer a bunch of flowers is to offer happiness!


Take a look at our selection of our bunches of flowers !
